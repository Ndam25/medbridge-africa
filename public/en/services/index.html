Our Services

MedBridge Africa provides practical support to health professionals and institutions aiming to work, collaborate or relocate internationally. We focus on:

  • Immigration assessments — EB-1A / EB-2 NIW pre-evaluations and strategy.
  • Grant writing & project design — proposals for research and implementation.
  • Training & capacity building — field epidemiology, nutrition, public health.
  • Partnerships & consulting — institutional collaborations and technical assistance.
