About MedBridge Africa

MedBridge Africa supports health professionals, institutions and SMEs across Africa with immigration advisory, capacity building and project design. We combine clinical expertise, public health experience and project management to help our clients increase their international collaborations and impact.

What we do

  • Eligibility assessments (EB-1A, EB-2 NIW)
  • Training & workshops (epidemiology, nutrition, public health)
  • Grant writing and proposal development
  • Institutional consulting and partnership building
